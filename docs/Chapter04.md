**GLOBAL DIGITAL PRINT PRODUCTION EQUIPMENT ENCYCLOPEDIA (GDPPE)**

**Master Research & Data Collection Specification**

**Chapter 4 -- Global Manufacturer Directory & Research Coverage**

**4.1 Purpose**

The purpose of this chapter is to establish a standardized framework for
identifying, classifying, researching, and maintaining every
manufacturer that participates in the global digital print production
industry.

Unlike conventional product directories, GDPPE will maintain a **living
manufacturer database** that captures not only current manufacturers but
also discontinued brands, acquired companies, OEM manufacturers,
regional suppliers, and private-label brands.

The Manufacturer Directory is the backbone of the encyclopedia. Every
equipment record, software package, printhead, ink system, and material
will be linked to a manufacturer profile.

**4.2 Objectives**

The Global Manufacturer Directory shall:

-   Maintain a master list of manufacturers.

-   Record corporate history and ownership.

-   Track mergers and acquisitions.

-   Identify OEM relationships.

-   Document manufacturing locations.

-   Record product portfolios.

-   Track discontinued brands.

-   Link manufacturers to machine models.

-   Support advanced search and filtering.

-   Enable future business intelligence reporting.

**4.11 Manufacturer Status**

Every manufacturer shall be classified as:

-   Active

-   Active (Limited Production)

-   Acquired

-   Merged

-   Rebranded

-   Discontinued

-   Defunct

-   Under Restructuring

This ensures historical products remain correctly attributed.

**4.12 Manufacturer Naming Standards**

To maintain consistency:

-   Always use the official registered company name on first reference.

-   Record common brand names as aliases.

-   Preserve historical names where applicable.

-   Avoid abbreviations unless officially used by the manufacturer.

Example:

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Official Name**                                  **Common Brand**
  -------------------------------------------------- --------------------
  Canon Production Printing Netherlands B.V.         Canon Arizona

  Zünd Systemtechnik AG                              Zünd

  Durst Phototechnik AG                              Durst
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**4.14 Chapter Summary**

The Global Manufacturer Directory establishes the corporate foundation
of the GDPPE. Every equipment record in the encyclopedia will be linked
to a verified manufacturer profile, enabling accurate product
attribution, historical tracking, OEM relationship mapping, and future
expansion. By standardizing manufacturer research and classification,
GDPPE ensures consistency across all categories while supporting
engineering research, procurement analysis, and knowledge graph
relationships.
