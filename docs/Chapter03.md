**CHAPTER 3**

**Global Equipment Taxonomy & Classification System**

**Version 1.0**

**3.1 Purpose**

The purpose of this chapter is to establish a universal classification
system for all equipment documented within the **Global Digital Print
Production Equipment Encyclopedia (GDPPE).**

This taxonomy provides a standardized framework for organizing,
researching, comparing, indexing, and presenting digital print
production equipment regardless of manufacturer, country of origin, or
product generation.

Rather than grouping equipment by manufacturer, GDPPE organizes
equipment according to **production technology and manufacturing
workflow**, allowing direct comparison between competing systems.

The taxonomy serves as the foundation for:

-   Research

-   Equipment identification

-   Engineering documentation

-   Product comparison

-   HTML navigation

-   Knowledge Graph

-   AI search

-   Excel database

-   API development

-   Future ERP integration

**3.2 Classification Philosophy**

Equipment shall always be classified according to its primary production
function.

The hierarchy shall follow:

Production Technology

↓

Equipment Category

↓

Equipment Type

↓

Manufacturer

↓

Product Family

↓

Series

↓

Model

↓

Generation

↓

↓

Options

production tasks regardless of brand.

**Chapter Summary**

This chapter establishes the classification framework that will organize
every machine, component, software product, and material within GDPPE.
By using a technology-first hierarchy, the encyclopedia will support
meaningful comparisons, scalable expansion, and future AI-powered search
capabilities while remaining intuitive for engineers, production
managers, and procurement professionals. It also introduces an
18-category taxonomy that covers the complete digital print production
ecosystem from printing through finishing, software, consumables, and
factory automation.
